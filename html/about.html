ABOUT

A good day at work as a professional photographer can be an extraordinary one.

I’ve hiked in the Rocky Mountains to photograph researchers, flown in a helicopter for aerial photos, captured images of a cowboy riding bull, and sang-along while I took photos at a live concert. Moments like these, and all the interesting people I’ve met along the way, have kept me motivated throughout my award-winning photography career.

Not content to rest on my laurels, I expanded my photography to teaching by creating a successful YouTube channel with over 700,000 views, and The Open Source Photography Course -a unique online photography course for Linux users.

More recently, I’ve been learning to code JavaScript, jQuery, and ReactJS for front-end web development.
